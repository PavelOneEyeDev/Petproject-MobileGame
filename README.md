# Petproject-MobileGame
A petproject on Unity 2D on the topic of Educational Visual Novel

A practice in developing on the Unity engine. Objective: a visual novel that teaches information systems and technologies.
This game is designed to teach basic knowledge of "information systems and technology". As you play, you'll explore the world of information with your loyal companion, Tutorashka. Together, you'll face various challenges and gain a deeper understanding of information systems and technology.
